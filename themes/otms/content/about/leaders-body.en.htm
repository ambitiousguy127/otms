• Mirek Dabrowski

    Co-founder & CEO

    Mirek conceived the idea of Transport Connected – the mission of oTMS that he co-founded with his partner David Duan in 2011. oTMS connects manufacturers, retailers, transport providers and drivers into highly automated, digitized deliveries ecosystem to make transportation truly competitive.
    Mirek has over 18 years experience in leading global logistics providers and he has been based in China since 2000. He arrived here with Maersk as a management trainee and held there various roles until 2004 when he joined DHL Supply Chain. Mirek built sales and commercial team there and held the role of VP Business Development Greater China until 2008 when he joined DSV, one of leading European trucking and forwarding providers. At DSV Mirek was GM of DSV Logistics China until end 2012 where he tripled the revenues and substantially expanded company warehousing and trucking activities into 12 cities and 150 full time staff.

  • David Duan 段琰

    Co-founder & President

    David is a seasoned manager/marketer and is the the co-founder as well as COO of oTMS.cn. He has held various key roles in Business Development and Marketing in the contract logistics and transportation sector with DSV, DHL, and ST-Anda. His unique blend of expertise in transportation solution planning and cost control, extensive marketing and sales experience, and technical aptitude make him an effective candidate in tackling the current market inefficiencies of the Chinese trucking industry. David possesses a rare eye for this rapidly shifting vista and can be found constantly searching for ways to stay ahead of his competitors. While at DSV as the Head of Logistics Business Development, he was responsible for expansion in the entire pan-China area across the technology, fashion, automotive, and manufacturing sectors. Before that, he was Business Development Manager at DHL Supply Chain and focused mainly on FMCG, retail, and healthcare. David has spoken at various conferences and forums and holds degrees in International Economics and Supply Chain Management.

  • Zhiqi Zhang 张志琦

    Senior VP

    Zhiqi joined oTMS as VP of sales and is in charge of all Presales, Sales, and Channel team. Zhiqi has 16 years of experience in senior management in large international enterprises and is instrumental in helping oTMS transform the logistic landscape through innovative tech-thinking. Previously at SAP, he was VP of sales / GM of East & Central China and lead a 180+ Presales team responsible for five major products ( Solution Plan for Enterprise Application, Database and Technology Platform, Business Intelligence, Mobile Application, & Cloud Computing.) Having held a multitude of leadership positions and roles as Architect, Manager, and Speaker, he has expertise in Sales, Product, and Operations across a wide spectrum of markets ranging from retail to consumer to energy & chemical to financial and public services. Zhiqi holds a degree in Automation.

  • Peter Liu 刘展雄

    Senior VP

    Peter brings a wealth of experience to oTMS as VP / Regional Manager of South China District overseeing Sales, Strategy, and Operations.   • David Qi 齐炜

    R&D Head

    David joined oTMS in early 2015 as head of Research & Development. With more than 10 years of technical and management experience in domestic and offshore enterprise software working with up to 250 cross-functional teams, David is responsible for aligning engineering teams across all core products.
